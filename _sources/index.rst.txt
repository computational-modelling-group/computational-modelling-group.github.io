Support and consultancy
-----------------------

For support and consultancy for Computational Modelling, High Performance
Computing and Research Software Engineering, the following support structures
and points of contact are available:

- For High Performance Computing, please approach the `[TODO: insert link] High
  Performance Computing <https://isolutions.soton.ac.uk/hpc>`__ group. This
  includes access to and usage of the University's high performance computing
  facilities.

- For Research Software Engineering (RSE) advice and support, please contact the
  `[TODO: insert link] Research Software Engineering Group <https://rse.soton.ac.uk>`__ at the
  University of Southampton. This includes planning of grant proposals that have
  a computational or data component and would like to include staff time from
  RSE experts.

- For other queries, including consultancy requests from outside the university,
  contact `Hans Fangohr by email <mailto:fangohr@soton.ac.uk>`__.
